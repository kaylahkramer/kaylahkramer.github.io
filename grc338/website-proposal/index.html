The Trendy Cheapstake

The Trendy Cheapskate is a low-budget clothing company that capitalizes on the rising trend of sustainability and individuality, while saving our customers a quick penny. Fashion shouldnâ€™t be defined by the wealthy. Fashion should be defined by you.

Target audience

The Trendy Cheapskate reaches a young, artistic and broad range of diverse individuals coming from varying socio-economic backgrounds.

The target audience for The Trendy Cheapskate understands the value of a dollar as well as the importance of individual expression. Each customer will have a unique sense of style and an intuitive ability to navigate through technology.
